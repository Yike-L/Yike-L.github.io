Hi, I’m Yike.

I am currently a Ph.D. student at George Mason University. Previously, I majored Information Art and Design at Tsinghua University and received a master’s degree in Computer Science and Technology, under the guidance of Professor Song-Hai Zhang. During my undergraduate years, I majored in Computer Science and Technology at Wuhan University of Science and Technology.

My research focuses on computer graphics, virtual reality, and human-computer interaction.
